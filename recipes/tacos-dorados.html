Tacos Dorados

Description

Today we have a simple yet very delicious mexican recipe: tacos dorados. Its not a complcated recipe and it can be prepared relatively quickly. The ingredients are easy to get a hold of from any supermarket.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tortillas
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheese
  • Avocados
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Break apart the chicken into small bits and roll them up with tortillas
  2. Heat up a deep pan and add enough oil to cover the base of the tacos
  3. Do not leave while they are frying. Roll the tacos over until they are golden
  4. Add toppings of your choice
